Sunny

Clear skies with abundant sunlight and no significant cloud cover or precipitation.



Cold Front

A boundary between cold, dense air and warm, less dense air, often resulting in cooler temperatures and weather changes.


Hailstorm

Precipitation in the form of hail, consisting of ice pellets that can vary in size and potentially cause damage.


Rainy

Continuous or intermittent rainfall with varying intensities, often accompanied by overcast skies.



Cloudy

Overcast skies with limited sunlight, but no active precipitation.

Foggy

Reduced visibility due to the presence of suspended water droplets near the ground, often creating a misty or foggy atmosphere.

Thunderstorm

A storm characterized by lightning, thunder, heavy rain, and sometimes hail or strong winds.

Snowy

Falling snowflakes or snow cover on the ground, typically associated with cold temperatures.
